About Us



Our Goal



To Empower
Famers and Digitalize
the Middle-Men role so as to
Disseminate Farm Produce
at a Cheaper
Rate;



Our Vision



To Help
Local Farmers Sell
Their Farm Produce Directly
and Profitably to Consumers;
Who gets Fresh Farm Produce
at a Cheaper Rate by Digitalizing
the Middle-Men.



Our Mission



To Create
a Technology Solution
that will Help Farmers to
sell profitably and also give Consumers
The Opportunity to buy
at a Cheaper Rate.



Problem Statement



Middlemen tend
to rip farmers off
by buying so cheaply from them
and selling high to consumers.
FarmMarket aims to address that,
by enabling farmers to sell products directly to
consumers through the use of technology.
this helps them get more value
for their efforts while making
the product cheaper for
Consumers.
